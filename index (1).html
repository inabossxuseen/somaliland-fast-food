Our Story

Feeding Somaliland Since 2015

Somaliland Fast Food was born from a simple dream — to bring world-class fast food flavours to the heart of Hargeisa. What started as a single street-corner kitchen has grown into Somaliland's most-loved fast food brand, serving thousands of happy customers every single day.

We source the freshest ingredients, hand-prepare every item to order, and deliver with lightning speed. From our signature double-smash burger to our stone-baked pizzas and slow-marinated shawarma, every bite tells a story of passion and quality.
